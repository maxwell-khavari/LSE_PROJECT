Session 01

Initial intake

Clarify your goals, expectations, and purpose for this process.

1What would you like to be different?
2What obstacles exist, and what will help you overcome them?
3What stands out to you right now, and why?
4What are your best hopes for our time together?
5What goals would you like to achieve?
6What will you contribute yourself as part of this process?
7What do you predict will happen over the next few weeks?
Community

Session 02

Community exploration

Community is belonging, opportunity, and hidden connection.

1What words come to mind when you hear "community"?
2What does community mean to you?
3What makes an ideal community?
4What groups have impacted your life most?
5Where do you feel most like yourself?
6Who do you see most often? Has this changed?
7If everything changed tomorrow, who would still be around?
